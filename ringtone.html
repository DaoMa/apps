Ringtone Maker - make free ringtones for iPhone

You can make free ringtones from music in your library and record your sound as ringtones for free in this app. Now you can make rongtones from youtube videos or tiktok videos!

Features::
* Support call ringtone,text tones,mail alter tones,twitter alter tones and calendar tones.
* Convert your iTunes music to ringtone for free,support mp3 and other format music.
* Create unlimited ringtones(no time limit), up to 40 seconds.(no length limit)
* Record sound and save as ringtone
* Share ringtones to your friends by mail
